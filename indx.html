inSource Do you write a post excerpt? This is a short two or three sentence summary that hooks the reader in to your post. When someone searches in Google for, “The best Chili recipe ever…” you want the search engine to pull up your blog post title plus a great description that gets them to click on your link. If you haven’t written an excerpt, it will automatically pull the first 55 words of your post (which may or may not be the best choice).

Excerpts will also appear in google readers when you’ve got “summary” checked off in the reading settings.
